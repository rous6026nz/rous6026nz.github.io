What is your experience of compassionate (or not) leadership?

I have had a number of compassionate managers throughout my career. My first manager who showed genuine compassion was when I was working for an automotive repairs shop.

He was relatable and approachable, he treated every team member equally, scheduled and prioritised 1-on-1 time and showed genuine care and interest in each and every team member.

Working for a manager who displayed genuine compassion and care for the people around them, resulted in a pleasant working environment and a high performing team willing to go that extra mile to exceed targets and expectations.
